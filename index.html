A poem for you: A portable paradise

And if I speak of paradise

I speak of a land far away

Where imagination and creativity

Combine together

Where big screens and controllers

Are lying on the ground

And where brand new video games

Are stacked on the shelves

And then when you'd move on

You'd change it altogether

And hear soothing music

Coming from a piano

Where you can feel the marbly keys

Pressing right down

Where guitar strings would play

As if there were no tommorow

And then you'd move on

And smell sweet honey

Drizzling on a pancake

And you'd bite into it

And devour it right away

And then you'd smell a pizza

And choose your toppings

And you'd admire how it tastes

And if I speak of paradise

I speak of a land far away

Where imagination and creativity

Combine together
